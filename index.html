Regala Interactive Farming

How to Play

Welcome to your interactive farming experience! Follow these steps to grow and harvest crops:

🎮 Controls:

  • WASD - Move the player.
  • Mouse Click - Interact with the farm.
  • S (at the door) - Sleep to advance time.

🌱 Gameplay Steps:

  1. Click on a farmland plot to plant seeds.
  2. Once all plots are planted, click the water drop to water them.
  3. After watering, an arrow will point to the house – go inside!
  4. Stand at the door and press S to sleep.
  5. After the required days, crops will become ready to harvest.
  6. Click on the crops to harvest them!

Keep growing and collecting crops to continue farming! 🌾👨‍🌾
